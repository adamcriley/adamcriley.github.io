Voice of the Customer

The VoC shares feedback recorded during front-line encounters with stakeholders across the organization. The project began as a response to the decommissioning of the legacy system and required a ground-up redesign of every aspect of the process. We built a bespoke solution on top of existing enterprise softward with enhancements including global and fielded searching, automated alerts, and a back end automation that allowed for continuous data deployment.
